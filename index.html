In 10 Days We'll Give You Back Your Life

Sobriety can start right now. Because addiction is a compulsive physiological need for a habit-forming substanse, we understand that the person suffering from alcohol or drug addictions is suffering from a biopsychosocial disease that needs medical treatment. Our unique approach involves counter conditioning minimal sedation interviews, counseling, education and tools for support in sobriety. We help people beat addiction by training their brain to look at the substance in a different way by creating behavior modification with alcohol or dugs.
